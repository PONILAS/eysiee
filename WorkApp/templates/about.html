ABOUT US


Employment website for Job seekers


It is a website that deals specifically with employment or careers. Many employment websites are designed to allow employers to post job requirements for a position to be filled and are commonly known as job boards. Our websites offer employer reviews, career and job-search advice, and describe different job descriptions or employers. Through our website a prospective employee can locate and fill out a job application or submit resumes over the internet for the advertised position. Trabahunters will be the solution for the growing problems in job mismatch in our country. The mission of our website is to give jobs to those people whose unemployed and also helping them to find a job in a less hassle way.
